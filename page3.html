Mission

    Our mission is to make the message and medicine of self-motivation accessible and attainable to over million people worldwide so that within 3 generations we can create a world in which every people, especially the youth is motivated towards their goals in life, so that no matter what they face along their path in life they have the power within to stay true to themselves, trust themselves and to feel loved, supported, cared for, safe and strong.
        
   Our mission is to provide tools, practices, and wisdom through this website, to children, young people, and even adults. 

Vision

     Motivation is a kind of driving force that encourages an individual to get going. It is a kind of boost to the self confidence, faith and inner conscience of a person. All of us look for some kind of motivation in life. It is almost impossible to face a competition, achieve success of accomplish a goal without motivation.
        Through this website, the vision of  our team is to provide a new sense of purpose and direction to people's life.  To provide enthusiasm in people's life. To empower and encourage people to face tough times and competition in life with the help of self motivation.
